祛魅书籍｜Disenchanting the Book

试图以科学的方法祛魅书籍，

认真梳理知识、权力、书籍以及阅读的关系，

努力还原一名真实读者在历史中的模糊脸庞。

With rigorous methods, I seek to disenchant the book:

to patiently map the entanglements of knowledge, power, reading, and the book,

and to bring into focus the once-blurred face of real readers in history.
